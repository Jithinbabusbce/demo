Design Concept

  • Outer ring — The globe, representing "World" and global ambition
  • G letterform — Bold geometric "G" for Gully, the brand initial
  • Ring gap — Dynamic opening suggesting energy and forward motion
  • Horizontal bar — The crossbar of the G, grounding the mark with stability
  • Gold accent dot — A championship/trophy element evoking the cricket ball

The Green G

The Gully World mark is a bold geometric "G" constructed from a thick circular ring with a dynamic opening and horizontal bar. Every element carries meaning.

The circular form represents both a globe (World) and a cricket ball — the two foundational ideas of the brand. The thick ring conveys strength and permanence, while the open gap on the right side suggests forward motion, ambition, and accessibility.

The gold accent dot at the opening evokes a championship trophy element and the seam of a cricket ball — connecting the brand to competitive sports heritage.

Color choice: Forest green (#15803d) was chosen over brighter greens for its premium, authoritative quality. It evokes lush sports grounds while maintaining the gravitas expected of a platform aspiring to be a global brand.

Typography: DM Sans in Extra Bold (800) for "GULLY" provides impact and confidence. The tracked-out "WORLD" in Medium weight creates a sophisticated hierarchy — the bold G mark and bold "GULLY" command attention, while "WORLD" anchors the identity with understated elegance.
